## Purpose
Two *main* reasons;
1. Worlds' official servers are old, slowly deteriorating, and *probably unmaintained*.
2. Worlds is **old**. The shutdown of the servers is inevitable.

TLDR: To keep Worlds' legacy going for future generations.
